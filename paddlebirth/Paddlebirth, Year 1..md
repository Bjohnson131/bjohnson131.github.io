# Year 1
Ah, here we are, in a thick hot jungle. After months of travel the caravan has decided to place its roots here. I can only assume it's because of the fruit trees, and not because of the Giant brown recluse webs dotting the landscape. Speaking of... these smart dwarves have decided the best place to park the caravan is right smack next to one. Geniuses I tell you. ![[Screenshot_206.png]]

Trading in this is actually pretty neat. You send the items that you want to trade to the trade depot, and then you can send a trade envoy, it could be any dwarf, and they'll execute the trades you want.
![[Screenshot_210.png|694]]I bought a few barrels of milk. I think the dwarves will appreciate having a variety in their diet.

It's winter and the bedrooms are more or less done. you can see that I just left the stones in the rooms. The dwarves like them. It's worth noting that there is no such thing as a functional light source in Dwarf Fortress. This means anywhere in underground is treated the exact same as out in the sun. my preference is to think that dwarves live in the sheer darkness, and don't care.
